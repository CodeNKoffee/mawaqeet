Opening Mawaqeet on macOS

Mawaqeet isn't notarized by Apple yet, so the first time you open it macOS may say it "cannot be opened because Apple cannot check it for malicious software." That's expected — here's how to allow it:

  1. Open the .dmg and drag Mawaqeet into your Applications folder.
  2. Double-click Mawaqeet once (you'll see the warning — that's fine).
  3. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down to the Security section — you'll see a line about Mawaqeet being blocked. Click Open Anyway.
  5. Confirm with Open. Mawaqeet now launches normally every time.

Shortcut: instead of steps 2–5, right-click Mawaqeet in Applications → Open → Open.

Good to know (v0.1)

  • Emergency-exit passphrase: bismillah — in a strict block, click Emergency exit, hold the button, then type it to leave early. You can change it anytime in Settings → Focus blocker.
  • Known issue — multiple monitors: on multi-display setups the emergency-exit button can glitch and dismissing the blocker may not work reliably yet. On a single display it works as expected. A fix is on the way.
